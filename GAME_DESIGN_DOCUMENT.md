# Vision

The Island of Flames is a choice-based romance and mystery game where the player enters a tropical reality show unlike any other.

Players form friendships, build romances, uncover secrets, and investigate the mystery of the Crystal Flame while trying to survive life inside the villa.

Every decision matters.

# Core Gameplay

Players experience each episode through dialogue, choices, exploration, and relationship building.

Typical episode flow:

1. Opening cinematic
2. Villa life
3. Character conversations
4. Exploration
5. Challenge
6. Romance moments
7. Fire Pit Ceremony
8. Cliffhanger ending

---

# Main Story

You arrive on Flame Cay expecting a dating competition.

Instead, you discover the island hides an ancient mystery surrounding the Crystal Flame.

The deeper your relationships become, the more secrets begin to surface.
